*↘DocuSign Global Kick-Off

In this exhilarating virtual event, we embarked on a thrilling journey to revolutionize the future of digital experiences.

The challenge →

Cresci's Agency, in collaboration with Solis Collective, took the reins as the creative production agency, driving the creation of an awe-inspiring motion graphics branding campaign that captivated audiences worldwide.

The driving force behind DocuSign Global Kick-Off FY24 was to envision a virtual event that transcended boundaries and fostered an engaging, inclusive atmosphere for attendees. To achieve this, Cresci's Agency harnessed the power of motion graphics to infuse life into the event's branding. Our vision revolved around seamlessly blending innovative visuals with DocuSign's core values and global impact, ultimately delivering an unforgettable digital experience.

The beating heart of the event's identity lay within the bespoke motion graphics system.

Immersive
Storytelling

The motion graphics brought narratives to life, transforming presentations and key moments into captivating visual stories, thereby leaving a lasting impression on participants.
