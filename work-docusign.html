Friendly Illustrations

We connected with Supervielle’s team to craft 10 3D illustrations that would empathetically guide the user’s experience across the bank’s app.

Each illustration came to life after a delicate process of 3D modeling, texturing, and animation of the details that complement the communication.

The system

These illustrations complement Supervielle's Kite value system, which conveys friendliness, agility, and simplicity, so we used playing as the action and wind as the environment.
